## Description (4000 chars max)

```
Zikr is a beautiful, distraction-free dhikr counter built for Muslims who want to make remembrance of Allah a consistent daily practice.

Tap the orb. Count your dhikr. Build your streak.

── COUNTER ──
A calming, full-screen counter with a glowing tap orb at the center. Each tap is backed by subtle haptics. Switch between built-in dhikr presets — Tasbih, Tahlil, Salawat, Takbir, and Alhamdulillah — or create your own custom dhikr with Arabic text, transliteration, and a personal color.

Quick-add buttons (+1, +5, +10) let you catch up fast. Accidentally tapped? An undo banner appears for 5 seconds so you can correct it without losing your count.

── DAILY GOAL & PROGRESS ──
Set your personal daily target (default: 100). A gold progress ring tracks how close you are in real time. When you hit your goal, you feel it.

── STREAKS & REWARDS ──
Consistency is rewarded. Complete your daily goal to extend your streak. Longer streaks unlock XP multipliers:
• 3-day streak → 3x XP
• 7-day streak → 4x XP  
• 10-day streak → 5x XP

Earn badges as you grow:
🌟 First Light — complete your first daily target
🔥 Flame Streak — 3 consecutive days
✨ Week of Noor — 7 consecutive days
⚡ Barakah Overdrive — surpass your goal by 2x in a single day
⭐ Thousand Club — 1,000 total counts

── HISTORY ──
Review your daily counts, see which dhikr you recited most, and track your longest streaks. Every day logged is a step in your journey.

── SMART REMINDERS ──
Gentle nudges that respect your time. Set simple daily reminders, configure smart nudges (that automatically pause once you've hit your goal), or align reminders with your prayer times. Full control over Fajr, Dhuhr, Asr, Maghrib, and Isha.

── DESIGN ──
Royal blue, gold, and ivory — a palette inspired by the beauty of Islamic art. Full dark mode support follows your iPhone's system setting automatically.

── PRIVACY ──
Zikr stores all your data on-device. No account required. No data leaves your phone.

Start small. Stay consistent. Let every count be a reminder that you are not alone.

سبحان الله وبحمده
```

## Review Notes (for App Review team)
```
This is an Islamic dhikr (remembrance/prayer) counter app. No user accounts, no network requests, no external data collection. All data is stored locally on-device via UserDefaults. 

The app includes Arabic text and Islamic terminology (Tasbih, Tahlil, Salawat, Takbir, Alhamdulillah) which are common Islamic phrases used in prayer and remembrance.

There are no in-app purchases, subscriptions, or ads in this version.
```
